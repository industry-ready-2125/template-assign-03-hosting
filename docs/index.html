About Us!

 At Zeeshan, we serve authentic Hyderabadi cuisine prepared by Hyderabadi chefs at our 16 branches located in Pune, Visakhapatnam, Kakinada, Shrikakulam, Chennai, Tandur, Vikarabad, Mahabubnagar, and Narayanpet.


 We take great care in preparing each dish, using only the finest ingredients and traditional cooking techniques to deliver an authentic dining experience that is second to none. Our friendly staff and cozy ambiance at all our Branches are sure to make you feel Special. 


Our menu features Hyderabadi Dum Biryani, Kebabs, Curries, Sea Food, Hyderabadi Sweet Dish, and Arabian Cuisine Mandi. Come to Zeeshan and savor the true flavors of Hyderabad today.
